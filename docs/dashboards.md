# Dashboard Pages and Widgets

## 1. Executive Overview
Purpose: one-screen health view for a webinar/date range.

Widgets:
- spend
- landing visits
- registrations
- registration rate
- live attendees
- attendance rate
- replay viewers
- replay rate
- signups/purchases
- conversion rate from registration to purchase
- CAC / cost per registration / cost per attendee / cost per purchase
- funnel step chart
- daily trend chart

## 2. Acquisition Performance
Purpose: compare paid sources and campaign layers.

Widgets:
- table by source/campaign/ad set/ad
- spend, impressions, clicks, CTR, CPC
- registrations
- CPL
- attendees
- cost per attendee
- purchases
- CPA
- ROAS if revenue exists
- top creatives / worst creatives

## 3. Webinar Performance
Purpose: compare webinars/cohorts.

Widgets:
- webinar list with key metrics
- attendance by webinar/session/date
- watch duration distribution
- replay lift vs no replay
- conversion by webinar topic/host/date

## 4. Email Nurture
Purpose: see whether email sequences drive attendance and conversion.

Widgets:
- sends / opens / clicks by campaign or automation
- registration-to-attendance by nurture cohort
- last clicked email before attendance/purchase
- unsubscribe rate

## 5. Conversion / Revenue
Purpose: understand downstream business impact.

Widgets:
- signups/purchases by source/campaign/webinar
- revenue by source/campaign/webinar
- purchase lag from registration
- cohort table: registered -> attended -> purchased

## 6. Lead Timeline / Debug View
Purpose: inspect one lead/person.

Widgets:
- search by email/external ID
- event timeline
- attributed source/campaign
- webinar registrations and attendance details
- email interactions
- purchase events

## 7. Data Health
Purpose: trust the numbers.

Widgets:
- connector status cards
- last sync time
- last successful sync time
- rows ingested today
- sync errors
- unmapped/duplicate records
- registrations missing UTM/source

## Filtering
Global filters should support:
- date range
- webinar
- source
- campaign
- country
- device
- product/offer
